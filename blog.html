March 15th, 2022

finally announced album 2! "love, from the trees and the sun" will be out April 8th! i've had these files on my computer for a long while now, so i'm excited to get them shared with you all. the new single is out too - "you could always see the sun set from this hill" is up on all streaming services, bandcamp, etc. - of all things i took a lot from that one Postal Service song to make the backing arp. it's a pop song kinda! it combined with "it's getting late again", the previous single, gives you a good idea of what the album will sound like. a bit folky, a bit electronic, a bit ambient, a bit etc. i think it's really good though, and i hope you agree. don't want to let yall down after a year and a half!
